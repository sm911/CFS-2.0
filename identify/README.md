# IDENTIFY (ID) - Secondary Category

The Identify function helps organizations understand their cybersecurity risks to systems, people, assets, data, and capabilities.

## Subcategories

### ID.AM - Asset Management
Identifying and managing assets (devices, systems, data, software, services) to enable effective risk management.

### ID.RA - Risk Assessment
AI-driven threat landscape analysis, emerging attack vectors, vulnerability assessment, risk scenario planning.

### ID.IM - Improvement
Using lessons learned and predictive indicators to improve cybersecurity processes and activities.
