# RECOVER (RC) - Secondary Category

The Recover function identifies appropriate activities to maintain plans for resilience and to restore capabilities or services that were impaired due to a cybersecurity incident.

## Subcategories

### RC.RP - Incident Recovery Plan Execution
Recovery planning, restoration procedures, business continuity activation, disaster recovery operations.

### RC.CO - Incident Recovery Communication
Recovery status updates, stakeholder communications, lessons learned sharing, post-incident reporting.
